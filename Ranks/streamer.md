## Streamer perks

### Commands

- /ptime - Change your personal time

- /gmsp - Go into spectator

- /gms - Go into survival

- /v - Go into vanish

- /fly -  Fly!

- /map hide - Hide your location on the map

- /map show - Show your location on the map
